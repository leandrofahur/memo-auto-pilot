## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering a welcoming and respectful community, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Showing empathy and kindness
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
